Instructions

Use the control panel on the right to adjust the parameters of Torus Knot.

How to use:

  • Use the control pannel to adjust the number of twists and loops.
  • You can also change the radius and tubular segments of the shape.
  • And control rotation speed of the X & Y axis to your liking.
  • You may use any of the sliders or input a number in the textbox besides each entry.
  • To change the color of the shape just use the color picker or insert the colors hexcode.
